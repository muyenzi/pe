best pets

  • Parakeets

    • Parakeets make excellent pets for kids.These little birds pack in personality as colorful as their feathers.

  • Rats

    • According to Healthline, “Rats make excellent pets due to their intelligence, larger size, and enjoyment of human companionship.”

  • Hamsters

    • Animal Planet suggests waiting until your child is a little older before getting a hamster. For one, they are small in stature, making them a bit fragile in young hands.
